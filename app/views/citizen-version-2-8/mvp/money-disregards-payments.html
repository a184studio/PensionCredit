Some payments you get will not affect your Pension Credit

These include payments:

  • to help you live independently in your own home or the community
  • to correct or compensate for an official error or for arrears of benefits
  • related to Council Tax Reduction
  • to help you if you have been involved in an incident or emergency
  • from a home insurance policy to cover lost or damaged personal possessions
  • to support you with the effects of a blood infection or vCJD
  • for personal injury claims
  • related to compensation from the second world war or service in the armed forces
  • from the Windrush Compensation Scheme
