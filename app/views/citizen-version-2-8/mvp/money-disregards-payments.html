Some money will not affect your Pension Credit

This includes payments and payouts such as:

  • help for you to live independently in your own home or the community
  • corrections or compensations for an official error or for arrears of benefits
  • errors in Council Tax Reduction
  • help for you if you have been involved in an incident or emergency
  • home insurance policies to cover lost or damaged personal possessions
  • support for you with the effects of a blood infection or vCJD
  • personal injury claims
  • compensation related to the second world war or service in the armed forces
  • the Windrush Compensation Scheme
