We can ignore some of the money you have in some situations

We might not use some money, savings and investments to work out your Pension Credit if they are:

  • payments to help you live independently in your own home or the community
  • payments to correct or compensate for an official error or for arrears of benefits
  • payments related to Council Tax Reduction
  • payments to help you if you have been involved in an incident or emergency
  • payments from a home insurance policy to cover lost or damaged personal possessions
  • payments to support you with the effects of a blood infection or vCJD
  • personal injury claim payments
  • compensation payments related to the second world war or service in the armed forces
  • payments from the Windrush Compensation Scheme
