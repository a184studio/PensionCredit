Any other money held in this country or abroad @Pete / Talk to Dave

Treat as the type of account it is, though we also need to know the currency. These are converty by using XE Currency converter (https://www.xe.com/currencyconverter/)

For each type of money, saving or investment, ask the user for all relevant details, including:

  • the amount
  • the name of the account holder
  • the name on the bank or building society accounts
  • the name of the bank, building society or investment company
  • the last 4 digits of the account number
  • the start and end dates of any investment vehicles
  • any changes to amounts that the claimant expects during any backdating period
  • any land or property the claimant owns
  • any significant changes that the claimant expects over the next 12 months
