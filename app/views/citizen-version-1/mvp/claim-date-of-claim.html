You can backdate your claim for up to 3 months if you are eligible. You can claim up to 4 months in advance if you have not yet reached State Pension age.

Things that might affect your eligibility are:

  • previous claims for Pension Credit or other benefits
  • changes to whether you live with a partner or anyone else
  • changes to the amount of money you have, your income or employment status
  • whether you have spent longer than 4 weeks outside the UK in the period you are claiming for, unless it was connected to medical treatment, convalescence or the death of a partner or child
