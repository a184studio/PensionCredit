Information you will need to apply online

This service is for new Pension Credit applications only. If you are already getting Pension Credit you should call us to tell us about a change of circumstances.

If you do not enter anything for more than 30 minutes, your application will time out and you will have to start again. This is to keep your details secure. You cannot save your application and come back later.

How we will use your information

As well as the details you provide in your application, we will also use information that DWP and HMRC know about you (and your partner, if you live with one).

We will not need to ask you about things we already know, such as:

  • your UK State Pension and any benefits you receive from DWP
  • your UK income and earnings

We will need to ask you about (for you and your partner, if you live with one):

  • National Insurance number
  • information about UK personal pensions you have not taken yet
  • information about income and pensions from overseas
  • details of money, savings and investments, usually for the last 3 months
