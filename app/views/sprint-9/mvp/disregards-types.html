Do you have any money that should not be part of you Pension Credit claim?

  • Charity payments
    Ex-gratia or compensation payments from charities or trusts.

  • Home insurance
    Payments to cover loss or damage to personal possessions

  • Money for home repairs
    Money for essential repairs to the home you live in, like roof repairs.

  • Money to help you live independently in your own home
    Money to adapt your home with things like stair lifts, ramps or accessible bathrooms.

  • Local authority payments
    Payments made to you for welfare assistance, which is money when you return from prision or a care home. (Each LA is different @dan to source a list) + X, Y and Z.

  • Money to buy your main home
    Money that you intend to use to buy the home you live in.

  • Personal injury insurance claims
    Payments made to you if you have been injured in an accident.

  • Other payments, grants or bursaries
