You must have the right information ready

You will not be able to save your application and come back later. So you need to make sure you have certain personal and financial information for you and your partner (if you have one) to hand while you make your application:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank statements, usually for the last three months
  • details of any income, pensions, savings or investments

To keep your information secure while making an application, if you do not enter any information for more than 30 minutes, your application will time out and you will have to start again.
